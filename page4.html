Tatsuki Fujimoto

Background The Manga's he's made
Tatsuki Fujimoto is a Japanese manga artist known for writting and drawing Fire Punch, Nayuta of the Prophecy, Goodbye Eri, and Chainsaw man.

Fujimoto started drawing at an early age. In his first Years of High school he started uploading mangas to a website called Shintosha. After he graduated he countinued to publish manga and started getting awards for them. One of the first Manga's that blew up for him was Fire Punch which ran for 8 volumes before ending. After Fire Punch ended one of Fujimoto's newest work would gain so much attention that the manga and anime community wanted to see it animated. The Manga was called Chainsaw Man.

Chainsaw Man was a weekly Shonen Jump manga that was published in Japan in December 2018 and a lot of people enjoyed it. They enjoyed it to the point of it getting fan edits and people asking for an anime studio to make the first season. In October 12, 2022 the anime was made by MAPPA and was as popular as the manga. After Chainsaw Man ended he started making Chainsaw Man chapter two featuring a new main character. To move on from Chainsaw Man, one of his recent works is called "Goodbye Eri". Personally I haven't read it but many people have been saying it was good. I wouldn't doubt it either considering the man who makes it loves the work he does.
